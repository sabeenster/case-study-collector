📋 What to Collect for Case Studies ▸

Onboarding (Baseline)
  • What does the brand sell? Size? Support volume?
  • How many support agents? Where? Cost?
  • What's broken? What are they spending too much on?
  • Current CSAT, resolution time, first response time
  • What platforms? (Redo, Gorgias, Zendesk)
  • What does success look like for them?
During Deployment (Weeks 1-4)
  • What changed when AI agents went live?
  • How did existing staff respond?
  • Any immediate wins?
  • What needed tuning?
Ongoing (Monthly)
  • Same metrics as baseline for comparison
  • Anything the brand owner/team said that's quotable
  • Things you notice about how they use the system
  • Headcount changes, time saved, cost savings
Results (Case Study Ready)
  • Before/after metric comparison
  • ROI: cost savings vs. Agentway investment
  • Direct customer testimonial quote
  • Unexpected benefits they didn't expect
